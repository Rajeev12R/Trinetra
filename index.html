Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about Senerity and our services. If you have any other queries, feel free to reach out.

  • How do I chat with the AI doctor?

    To start a coversation with the AI Serenity, simply click on 'Chat with Serenity' section, choose your preferred doctor, select a time slot, and confirm your appointment.

  • What types of medical specialists are available on Senerity?

    We offer access to general physicians, dermatologists, cardiologists, dietitians, mental health specialists, and many more.

    Yes, we prioritize patient privacy and security. Your consultation data is encrypted and only accessible to you and your doctor.

    No hidden charges! The consultation fee is displayed upfront before you book an appointment.
